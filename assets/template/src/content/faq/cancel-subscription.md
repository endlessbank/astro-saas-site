You can cancel your subscription anytime with no penalties or hidden fees.

## How to Cancel

1. Log into your {{SAAS_NAME}} account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**
4. Confirm your cancellation

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

## What Happens After Cancellation?

- You keep access until your paid period ends
- Your data remains available for 30 days
- You can export your data anytime before deletion
- You can reactivate anytime

## Pausing Instead of Canceling

Need a break but planning to return? Contact support to discuss pausing your subscription instead.

## Refund Policy

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you cancel within 14 days of your first payment, you'll receive a full refund.

## Questions?

Having issues canceling or want to discuss your options? Our support team is happy to help.
